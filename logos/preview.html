JobTracker — Logo Concepts

concept-1 — Briefcase + Checkmark
Classic job-search metaphor. Geometric briefcase with green check badge.
concept-2 — Pipeline Bars
Kanban-inspired funnel bars colored by application status (Applied → Offer).
concept-3 — JT Lettermark
Interlocking "J" and "T" monogram sharing a top bar. Bold, iconic, works at tiny sizes.
concept-4 — Target / Bullseye
Concentric rings with green center — "hitting your career goals". Clean and universal.
concept-5 — Network Nodes
Three connected nodes (Companies, Contacts, Skills). Green hub = you at the center.
